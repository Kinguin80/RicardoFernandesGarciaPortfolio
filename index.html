About

Ricardo Fernandes Garcia is an artist and Harvard student currently studying what he likes to acronymize as HALFVS: half Art, Film, and Visual Studies (AFVS), and half History and Literature (H&L). His nationality is similarly constructed by two halves: he is Venezuelan-Portuguese, though he resides in the oven-like Orlando, FL—making him especially delighted to bask in Massachusetts's below-100°F weather.
