# Image Folders

This folder contains images organized by series. Each series has its own folder.

## Series Folders

- `anthropocene/` - Anthropocene series
- `chronophobia/` - Chronophobia series
- `cognitive-dissonance/` - Cognitive Dissonance series
- `encoded/` - */Encoded/* series
- `explorador/` - Explorador series
- `freshman/` - Freshman series
- `graphic-design/` - Graphic Design series
- `memoria/` - Memoria series
- `modern-ruins/` - Modern Ruins series
- `muralwork/` - Mural work
- `other-works/` - Artworks without a series
- `penguin/` - Penguin series
- `postermania/` - Postermania series
- `technomancy/` - Technomancy series
- `vanished-spaces-banished-faces/` - Vanished Spaces/Banished Faces series

## Naming Convention

### Main Images
- Filename: `artwork-title.jpg` (lowercase, hyphens)
- Example: `bleached-dreams.jpg`

### Thumbnails
- Filename: `artwork-title-thumb.jpg` (lowercase, hyphens)
- Example: `bleached-dreams-thumb.jpg`

### How Titles Convert to Filenames
- "Bleached Dreams" → `bleached-dreams.jpg`
- "Where do I Put My Time?" → `where-do-i-put-my-time.jpg`
- "Petroleum Poverty" → `petroleum-poverty.jpg`

## Adding Images

1. **Find the artwork's series** from your Google Sheet
2. **Convert the title** to lowercase with hyphens
3. **Add files** to the matching series folder:
   - Main: `artwork-title.jpg`
   - Thumb: `artwork-title-thumb.jpg`

## Example

Artwork: "Bleached Dreams" (Series: "Explorador")
- Main image: `assets/images/explorador/bleached-dreams.jpg`
- Thumbnail: `assets/images/explorador/bleached-dreams-thumb.jpg`

## Tips

- Use JPG or PNG format
- Optimize images for web (aim for <500KB each)
- Thumbnails: 400-600px width recommended
- Main images: 1200-2000px width recommended
